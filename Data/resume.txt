Summary:
A brief statement that highlights your skills, experience, and career goals. Keep it concise and to the point.

Experience:
List your previous jobs in reverse chronological order, starting with your most recent position. Include the company name, your title, the dates you worked there, and a list of your responsibilities and achievements in bullet points.

Education:
List your degrees in reverse chronological order, starting with your most recent degree. Include the name of the degree, the name of the institution, and the dates you attended.

Skills:
List any relevant skills that you have that would be beneficial to the job you are applying for. This could include things like proficiency in a certain software, language skills, or other relevant abilities.

References:
Include a list of references or a statement that references are available upon request.
